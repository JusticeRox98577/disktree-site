Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2025

30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not happy with DiskTree Pro for any reason, email us within 30 days of purchase and we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.

How to Request a Refund

Email support@disktree.org with your order number (found in your Paddle receipt email) and we'll process your refund within 1–3 business days.

Eligibility

  • Requests made within 30 days of purchase are always approved
  • Requests after 30 days are considered on a case-by-case basis
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method via Paddle

After a Refund

Your Pro license key will be deactivated after a refund is processed. You can continue using the free version of DiskTree.

Contact

Questions? Email support@disktree.org.
