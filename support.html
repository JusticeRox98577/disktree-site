Common questions

How do I activate Pro?

Open DiskTree, click the "Free — Upgrade" badge in the sidebar, enter the email address you used at checkout, and click Activate. Pro features unlock instantly.

I can't open the app — macOS says it's damaged or from an unidentified developer.

Right-click the installer and choose Open, then click Open in the dialog. This only needs to be done once. Alternatively, go to System Settings → Privacy & Security and click "Open Anyway."

DiskTree is asking for permissions — is that safe?

Yes. DiskTree needs Full Disk Access to scan your drive. Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security → Full Disk Access and enable DiskTree. Your files never leave your Mac.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Log in to your Paddle account at my.paddle.com to manage or cancel your subscription. You can also email us and we'll handle it for you.

I want a refund.

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. Email support@disktree.org and we'll process it within 1 business day.

My subscription is active but Pro isn't unlocking.

Make sure you're entering the exact email you used at checkout. Check for typos and try lowercase. If it still doesn't work, email us with your order confirmation and we'll sort it out.
